# Brand and Language Guide

> **Anime Calendar v2 is an open-source Anime Release Intelligence Platform dedicated to accuracy, transparency, personalization, and long-term maintainability.**

This guide defines how the project communicates. Brand is not merely visual identity; it is the consistent expression of trust.

## Voice

The project voice is:

- clear;
- calm;
- factual;
- welcoming;
- transparent about uncertainty;
- technically precise without being needlessly difficult;
- enthusiastic without exaggeration.

## We say

- “Confirmed by the official provider.”
- “Reported date; regional availability is not yet known.”
- “Estimated with medium confidence.”
- “Streaming provider unknown.”
- “No matching releases were found in this window.”

## We avoid

- “Guaranteed” when a source can change.
- “Official” without first-party evidence.
- “Available everywhere” without regional proof.
- “Coming soon” when no meaningful timeframe exists.
- urgency, hype, or clickbait language.
- presenting absence of data as absence of availability.

## Canonical terminology

Use these terms consistently:

- **Anime Release Intelligence Platform** — the overall product category.
- **Release** — a canonical availability or premiere record.
- **Streaming Provider** — a legal service or official distributor.
- **Release Type** — episode, movie, OVA, ONA, special, and related categories.
- **Version** — original, sub, dub, or another modeled edition.
- **Date Status** — confirmed, reported, estimated, or unknown.
- **Confidence** — verified, high, medium, low, or unknown.
- **Evidence** — the source and explanation supporting a claim.
- **Calendar Profile** — a saved collection of user filters that produces a feed.

Avoid using “platform” alone when “streaming provider” is intended.

## Confidence presentation

Confidence is meaningful only beside evidence. Do not use percentages unless generated by a documented model with calibrated interpretation.

- **Verified:** direct first-party evidence.
- **High:** strong structured or corroborated evidence.
- **Medium:** credible but incomplete or context-dependent evidence.
- **Low:** weak inference; show cautiously.
- **Unknown:** insufficient evidence.

## Visual direction

Future visual design should prioritize readability, accessibility, clear hierarchy, restrained provider branding, and consistent status indicators. Color must never be the only way information is communicated.

## Naming

The repository remains `anime-calendar-v2` during development. Public naming changes require documented product and migration review.
